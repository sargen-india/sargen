# Evolution of SARGEN™

## Purpose

This document records the evolutionary journey of SARGEN™ and preserves the major phases through which the framework developed.

## Evolution Timeline

### Phase 1 – Concept Formation

The initial ideas and observations that led to the creation of SARGEN™.

### Phase 2 – Governance Development

Definition of governance principles, accountability structures, and rule-led operation.

### Phase 3 – Automation Integration

Introduction of automation concepts aligned with governance and controlled execution.

### Phase 4 – Operational Implementation

Development of practical systems and frameworks supporting governed operations.

### Phase 5 – Public Documentation

Publication of foundational documentation and public repository resources.

## Evolution Principle

Creation Must Evolve.

Evolution should strengthen systems while preserving governance, accountability, and structural integrity.

## Preservation Note

Historical evolution records should be preserved as part of the permanent history of SARGEN™.
