# SARGEN™ Governance Diagram

Version: 1.0

Status: Reference Architecture

---

## Purpose

This document provides a simplified visual representation of the governance structure established within the SARGEN™ framework.

The diagram illustrates the relationship between governance, accountability, documentation, stewardship, continuity, and responsible evolution.

It is intended as a reference guide and does not replace any constitutional, governance, or operational document.

---

## Interpretation

### Governance

Governance establishes the rules, standards, and structures through which systems operate.

---

### Accountability

Accountability ensures that actions, decisions, and responsibilities remain reviewable and traceable.

---

### Documentation

Documentation preserves knowledge, standards, records, and decisions beyond individual participation.

---

### Stewardship

Stewardship maintains the framework, protects continuity, and supports responsible improvement.

---

### Continuity

Continuity preserves knowledge, responsibilities, and institutional memory across time.

---

### Responsible Evolution

Evolution allows adaptation and improvement while remaining consistent with governance, accountability, documentation, stewardship, and continuity.

---

## Architectural Principle

The structure is intentionally layered.

Governance establishes the framework.

Accountability, documentation, and stewardship operate as supporting pillars of continuity.

Continuity enables long-term preservation of knowledge, responsibility, and governance.

Responsible evolution is possible only when continuity remains protected.

All layers remain subordinate to the foundational principles established by the SARGEN™ Charter.
