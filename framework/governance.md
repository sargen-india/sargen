# SARGEN™ Governance Framework

Version: 1.0

---

## Purpose

Governance exists to ensure that systems operate according to defined rules rather than personal discretion.

The purpose of governance within SARGEN™ is to maintain consistency, accountability, continuity, and long-term sustainability.

Governance provides structure for decision-making while preserving the integrity of the framework.

---

## Governance Principle

Authority may execute.

Governance defines.

Rules guide.

Systems preserve.

Governance exists above operational activity and provides the standards under which operations occur.

---

## Rule-Led Structure

SARGEN™ follows a rule-led model.

Individuals may create, manage, operate, or inherit systems.

However, no individual exists above the governing framework.

Rules remain the primary source of authority.

---

## Governance Objectives

The objectives of governance are:

* Preserve framework integrity
* Maintain accountability
* Protect continuity
* Enable sustainable evolution
* Reduce dependence on individuals
* Ensure traceable decision-making

---

## Governance Hierarchy

The hierarchy of governance shall operate as follows:

1. Foundational Principles
2. Governance Rules
3. Operational Standards
4. Procedures
5. Individual Actions

Lower levels must remain consistent with higher levels.

No operational action should contradict foundational principles.

---

## Accountability

Governance requires accountability.

Actions affecting systems, assets, responsibilities, or governance structures should be capable of review and verification.

Accountability strengthens trust and system stability.

---

## Stewardship

Participants act as stewards rather than owners of governance.

Stewardship includes:

* Protecting framework integrity
* Maintaining documentation
* Preserving continuity
* Supporting responsible evolution

The responsibility of stewardship extends beyond immediate interests.

---

## Governance Changes

Governance structures may evolve.

Changes should:

* Be documented
* Include rationale
* Preserve historical records
* Maintain traceability

Governance evolution should strengthen the framework rather than weaken accountability.

---

## Independence of Governance

Governance should remain independent from temporary operational pressures whenever possible.

Short-term convenience should not override long-term sustainability.

---

## Long-Term Responsibility

Governance decisions should consider future consequences in addition to present outcomes.

SARGEN™ encourages decisions that support continuity across generations.

---

## Governance Objective

The ultimate objective of governance is the preservation of a stable, accountable, sustainable, and enduring system.
