# SARGEN™ Charter

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundational Charter

---

## Purpose

The purpose of SARGEN™ is to establish a framework that preserves continuity, governance, stewardship, accountability, and responsible evolution across time.

SARGEN™ exists to reduce dependence on individuals by embedding governance within documented structures, rules, records, and systems.

---

## Foundational Principle

**Systems should not depend on individuals; individuals should operate within governed systems.**

---

## Philosophy

**Creation Must Evolve.**

Evolution is necessary.

Continuity is essential.

Governance provides the structure through which both may coexist.

---

## Core Commitments

SARGEN™ is committed to:

* Governance
* Accountability
* Documentation
* Stewardship
* Continuity
* Responsible Evolution

---

## Governance Principle

Authority may execute.

Rules govern.

Systems preserve.

Stewards maintain.

---

## Stewardship Principle

Participants act as stewards rather than owners of governance.

Stewardship includes preservation, maintenance, documentation, continuity, and responsible improvement.

---

## Accountability Principle

Actions affecting governance, records, responsibilities, or continuity should be reviewable, traceable, and documented.

Accountability strengthens trust, consistency, and long-term system reliability.

---

## Documentation Principle

Documentation preserves knowledge beyond individual participation.

Records, decisions, standards, and governance structures should be maintained in a manner that supports continuity, transparency, and future stewardship.

---

## Continuity Principle

Knowledge, responsibilities, and records should remain accessible beyond any single individual, generation, or operational period.

---

## Evolution Principle

Change is permitted when it strengthens continuity, accountability, and long-term sustainability.

Evolution should be documented, traceable, and governed.

---

## Constitutional Integrity

The foundational principles of SARGEN™ constitute the constitutional basis of the framework.

No governance action, operational practice, amendment, stewardship decision, or future development should contradict these principles.

Governance shall remain subordinate to the foundational principles upon which SARGEN™ is established.

Future evolution is permitted only when it preserves continuity, strengthens accountability, respects stewardship, and remains consistent with documented governance.

No change shall invalidate the purpose of SARGEN™, undermine its continuity, or weaken its capacity for responsible evolution.

The preservation of constitutional integrity is a permanent responsibility of stewardship.
