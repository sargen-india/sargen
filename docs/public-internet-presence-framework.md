# SARGEN™ Public Internet Presence Framework

Version: 1.0

Status: Strategic Framework

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this framework is to establish a consistent, discoverable, and authoritative public presence for SARGEN™ across digital platforms.

Public presence supports visibility, continuity, credibility, and long-term preservation of the framework.

This framework defines how SARGEN™ should be represented in public digital environments.

---

## Strategic Objective

The objective is not popularity.

The objective is discoverability, consistency, and preservation.

Public presence should ensure that:

* SARGEN™ can be identified accurately.
* Official sources can be distinguished from unofficial sources.
* Historical records remain accessible.
* Future development remains traceable.
* Public references remain consistent.

---

---

## Authority Hierarchy

When multiple public references to SARGEN™ exist, authority shall be determined according to the following hierarchy.

### Primary Authority

Official SARGEN™ repositories, constitutional documents, governance frameworks, charter documents, whitepapers, and formally released publications.

These sources constitute the highest authority regarding the definition, governance, principles, and evolution of the framework.

### Secondary Authority

Official websites, documentation portals, knowledge bases, educational resources, and public materials maintained through recognized SARGEN™ stewardship.

These sources support and explain the framework but remain subordinate to Primary Authority.

### Supporting Authority

Articles, presentations, research references, educational discussions, community interpretations, and other external publications.

These sources may contribute understanding but do not constitute official authority.

### Conflict Resolution

In the event of inconsistency, contradiction, ambiguity, competing interpretations, or uncertainty regarding authority, the highest applicable authority shall prevail.

Where multiple Primary Authority sources exist, constitutional documents shall take precedence, followed by governance frameworks, charter documents, whitepapers, and other formally released publications.

Official constitutional and governance documents shall remain the definitive reference for the interpretation and evolution of the framework.
