# SARGEN™ Branding Framework

Version: 1.0

Status: Strategic Framework

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this framework is to establish a consistent and recognizable identity for SARGEN™ across all official publications, repositories, documents, communications, and future public platforms.

Branding supports continuity by preserving identity, recognition, and consistency across time.

---

## Branding Objective

The objective of branding is not promotion.

The objective is clarity, consistency, and identity preservation.

Branding should ensure that:

* Official materials are identifiable.
* Public references remain consistent.
* Terminology remains stable.
* Framework identity remains protected.
* Future stewardship remains aligned with established principles.

---

## Official Name

The official name of the framework is:

**SARGEN™**

Whenever practical, official publications should use the name consistently.

---

## Official Expansion

The official expansion of SARGEN™ is:

**System-Anchored, Rule-Led Governance & Evolving Network**

This expansion constitutes the canonical definition of the framework.

---

## Foundational Philosophy

The foundational philosophy of SARGEN™ is:

**Creation Must Evolve.**

This principle represents the framework's long-term approach to continuity, governance, stewardship, and responsible evolution.

---

## Identity Consistency Principle

Official documents should maintain consistent use of:

* Framework Name
* Official Expansion
* Core Terminology
* Governance Concepts
* Stewardship Concepts
* Constitutional References

Consistency strengthens recognition and continuity.

## Visual Identity Guidance

The visual identity of SARGEN™ may evolve over time through stewardship and documented governance processes.

Visual elements may include:

* Logo
* Wordmark
* Repository Header
* Website Header
* Document Covers
* Presentation Templates
* Other Official Visual Assets

Until formal visual identity standards are established, the official name **SARGEN™**, its canonical definition, and its documented frameworks shall constitute the primary identity of the framework.

Visual assets support identity.

They do not replace governance, constitutional authority, or official documentation.

Future visual identity standards should preserve continuity, recognizability, and consistency with the foundational principles of SARGEN™.


---

## Attribution Standard

When attribution is appropriate, the preferred format is:

**Powered by SARGEN™**

*System-Anchored, Rule-Led Governance & Evolving Network*

Alternative attribution formats may be used when context requires.

---

## Public Representation Principle

Public descriptions of SARGEN™ should remain consistent with:

* Constitutional Documents
* Governance Frameworks
* Charter Documents
* Whitepapers
* Official Repository Documentation

Public representation should preserve accuracy and avoid mischaracterization.

---

## Branding Authority

Official branding standards shall be governed through recognized SARGEN™ stewardship and constitutional processes.

Changes affecting identity, terminology, or official representation should remain documented and traceable.

---

## Preservation Principle

Branding contributes to continuity.

A consistent identity strengthens discoverability, attribution, historical preservation, and long-term recognition.

Identity should evolve responsibly while preserving foundational continuity.

---

## Long-Term Objective

The long-term objective of branding is to ensure that SARGEN™ remains identifiable, consistent, recognizable, and preservable across generations.

Recognition is beneficial.

Continuity is essential.
