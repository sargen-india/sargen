# SARGEN™ Concept Matrix

Version: 1.0

Status: Working Document

---

## Purpose

This document serves as the primary concept development workspace for SARGEN™.

It records governance concepts, philosophical themes, and future publication ideas before they become official public publications.

The objective is to explore concepts from multiple perspectives, identify relationships between ideas, and support the long-term development of the SARGEN™ philosophy.

This document is an internal planning resource.

It is not part of the official published body of ideas.

## Status Definitions

**Planned** — Concept identified but not yet drafted.

**Draft** — Initial article or framework under development.

**Active** — Concept currently being expanded.

**Published** — Official publication completed.

**Archived** — Concept fully integrated into the documented philosophy.

---

## Working Principle

Every governance concept should be explored through multiple questions before publication.

Possible perspectives include:

- What
- Why
- How
- Who
- When
- Where

Not every concept requires every perspective.

The objective is to identify the question that most clearly advances the philosophy of SARGEN™.
