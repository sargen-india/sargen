# SARGEN Founding Declaration

## Declaration

This document records the establishment of SARGEN and the principles upon which it was founded.

SARGEN was created to preserve continuity, stewardship, governance, and evolution across time.

It exists not as a temporary project, but as a structured system intended to endure beyond any single individual, generation, or period of activity.

---

## Founding Purpose

SARGEN was established to demonstrate that meaningful creations can be governed, maintained, improved, and transferred through defined systems rather than personal dependence.

Its purpose is to provide a framework through which continuity may be preserved while allowing responsible evolution.

The existence of SARGEN affirms the principle that creation should not end with its creator.

---

## Founding Principles

The foundation of SARGEN rests upon the following principles:

1. Continuity over disruption.
2. Stewardship over ownership.
3. Governance over personal authority.
4. Evolution over stagnation.
5. Responsibility over control.
6. Legacy over temporary achievement.

These principles guide all present and future actions undertaken in the name of SARGEN.

---

## Institutional Commitment

SARGEN commits itself to maintaining:

* A documented governance framework.
* A constitutional structure.
* Stewardship accountability.
* Succession continuity.
* Recorded institutional history.
* Responsible adaptation to future circumstances.

---

## Declaration of Continuity

The continuation of SARGEN shall not depend upon the presence of any particular individual.

Its structures, records, principles, and governance mechanisms are intended to support continuity across generations.

Future stewards are entrusted with preserving both the integrity and adaptability of the institution.

---

## Declaration of Evolution

SARGEN recognizes that all enduring systems must evolve.

Evolution is encouraged when it strengthens continuity, stewardship, governance, and long-term sustainability.

Change shall occur through established constitutional and governance processes.

---

## Legacy Statement

SARGEN is founded with the intention that its purpose, principles, and records may endure beyond its origin.

Each generation of stewardship inherits the responsibility to preserve what has been established and improve what can responsibly be improved.

---

## Founding Principle

**Creation Must Evolve.**

Through stewardship, continuity is preserved.

Through governance, authority is restrained.

Through succession, responsibility is transferred.

Through evolution, creation endures.
