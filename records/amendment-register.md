# Amendment Register

## Purpose

This register records all constitutional amendments adopted by SARGEN.

Its purpose is to preserve a permanent and transparent history of constitutional evolution.

---

## Amendment Recording Principles

An amendment record shall be created whenever:

* A constitutional amendment is proposed and adopted.
* An existing amendment is superseded.
* A constitutional provision is modified through authorized procedures.

Only amendments adopted through the constitutional amendment process may be recorded in this register.

---

## Amendment Record Format

Each amendment entry shall include:

* Amendment Number
* Title
* Adoption Date
* Status
* Summary
* Reference Document

## Constitutional Evolution Principle

The existence of amendments reflects the recognition that enduring institutions must evolve while preserving continuity and legitimacy.

---

## Historical Integrity

All adopted amendments shall remain recorded as part of the constitutional history of SARGEN.

Amendments may be superseded, but their historical record shall be preserved.

---

## Preservation Requirement

This register serves as the authoritative historical index of constitutional amendments.

The register shall be maintained by the responsible steward under the authority of the Constitution.
