# SARGEN Milestones Register

## Purpose

This register records significant events in the development, governance, evolution, and continuity of SARGEN.

Its purpose is to preserve institutional history and provide a chronological record of important milestones.

---

## Milestone Recording Principles

A milestone should be recorded when it:

* Establishes a new framework or governance structure.
* Creates or ratifies a constitutional document.
* Introduces a significant amendment.
* Marks a stewardship transition.
* Records a major institutional achievement.
* Represents a significant stage in the evolution of SARGEN.

---

## Milestone Format

Each milestone shall include:

* Milestone Number
* Date
* Title
* Category
* Summary
* Status

## Historical Integrity

Milestones shall be preserved as historical records.

Entries may be expanded with additional context, but previously recorded events shall not be removed without constitutional justification.

---

## Purpose of Preservation

The preservation of milestones enables future stewards to understand the development of SARGEN and the decisions that shaped its evolution.

Institutional memory is a component of continuity.
