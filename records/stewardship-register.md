# Stewardship Register

## Purpose

This register records the succession, appointment, service, and transition of stewards within SARGEN.

Its purpose is to preserve institutional continuity through an accurate historical record of stewardship.

---

## Stewardship Recording Principles

A stewardship record shall be created whenever:

* A founding steward is recognized.
* A successor steward assumes responsibility.
* A stewardship role is transferred.
* A stewardship term concludes.
* A constitutional succession event occurs.

---

## Stewardship Record Format

Each entry shall include:

* Steward Number
* Name or Designation
* Stewardship Role
* Start Date
* End Date (if applicable)
* Status
* Notes

## Continuity Principle

The stewardship register exists to preserve institutional memory and ensure transparent succession across generations of stewardship.

---

## Historical Integrity

Records within this register shall be preserved as part of the official historical record of SARGEN.

Entries may be supplemented with additional information but shall not be removed without constitutional authority.
